Corporate Catering

This type of catering is focused on providing meals for business events such as conferences, meetings, seminars, and office parties. It may include buffet-style meals, boxed lunches, or plated meals.

Wedding Catering

Wedding catering specializes in providing food and beverage services for weddings and related events. It often includes a customized menu, multi-course meals, and wedding cakes.

Event Catering

Event catering covers a wide range of social gatherings, such as birthdays, anniversaries, reunions, and gala dinners. The menu and service style can be tailored to suit the event's theme, whether it's a casual buffet or an elegant sit-down dinner.

Social Catering

Social catering refers to catering services for private parties and social gatherings, including house parties, holiday celebrations, and family gatherings. It can involve a variety of food options, from finger foods and appetizers to full-course meals.
